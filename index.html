Thank You.

We would like to thank all the creators who believed in Imagewiki. Our hope for a world in which creators can control the use of their works across the internet is not lost.

We still believe that the onus is on digital platforms to preserve the rights of creators from whom they so greatly benefit by querying a database of usage rules set by image owners. Until the United States adopts legislation in the spirit of EU Article 13, such a world is not likely to exist and we cannot sustain the costs of maintaining the platform until then.

We still maintain ownership of our reverse search and rights management technology. To partner with us, please reach out.
